About company

Let’s start a wild adventure together!

This is how it all started. We have always loved traveling and this had to lead somewhere.

Now, Tanzania is our second home.

Tanzania Travel is a travel agency run by the enthusiasts of Africa. In particular, Tanzania-lovers. Our adventure started when we decided to climb Kilimanjaro. We participated in some safari trips and the flash came. It was all about Tanzania – Tanzanian guides, Tanzanian wildlife, Tanzanian people and the whole experience. We definitely fell in love with the country. Just look at the people we work with on a daily basis – the indigenous tour guides with years of experience (see the Guides tab to learn more) – these will be people who will show you all the miracles of Tanzania during your trip. 

Why Tanzania?

You might not know it but Tanzania is famous for the most beautiful and the most interesting tourist spots in Africa. Even if the country might feel like an unexplored territory for some European travelers, it’s still worth discovering. Our tours give you a chance to have a brush with nature and Tanzanian national parks where an encounter with big-five animals is more than just an option. By ‘big five’ we mean lions, rhinos, buffalos, elephants and leopards. Also, this is a unique opportunity to see hippos, giraffes, antelopes and cheetahs in their natural environment. We provide you with an off-road pickup with a driver to enjoy the views from a safe place. Magnificent animals, picturesque views and the African flora with its enormous baobabs – this is something you really need to experience. There’s a chance you will go through the rushing rivers so… get prepared for something crazy!

Landscapes, wild animals, the African vegetation – Tanzania has even more to offer!

Tanzania is not just an unspoiled nature. On top of that, you’ll visit an indigenous African village where, seemingly, the time has stood still. Have you heard about the Maasai? They get dressed in traditional clothes and ornaments and live a very simple life. For people spending their lives in the western culture, this is an unforgettable experience. If you enjoy your time there (and we are 100% sure you do), you can also buy some souvenirs – mostly hand made by the local people. On top of these goodies, you will try the traditional Tanzanian cuisine – get prepared for some unique flavors to be found nowhere else. 

Tanzania is a very unique place in the world. It offers four amazing national parks you can explore during your safari trip. These are Serengeti, Tarangire, Krater Ngorongoro and Lake Manyara. The amazing fauna and flora await you here to be explored. We are certain you’ll find the encounter with African wildlife an unforgettable experience.

When you compare Tanzania to other African countries, you will see that Tanzania is a truly unique place. First, from the geographic point of view, it’s almost completely surrounded by water. Its west side is covered by the longest freshwater lake in the world – Tanganyika. In the north, there is the Victoria Lake and in the South, there is the oblong Malawi Lake.  On top of that, east Tanzania has access to the Indian Ocean. Do you need any further incentives to get amazed by this country?

Do you have any second thoughts? Still hesitating about joining an incredible safari trip? Time to make up your mind! Tanzania is the best place if you want to pursue adventure. Wild animals in their natural environment, gorgeous savannas, jaw-dropping fauna – this is something to remember and at the same time, this is something we want to give you as the most precious memory. We are impatiently waiting for you to reach out!
